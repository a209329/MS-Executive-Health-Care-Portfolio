Project Overview

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-being | Digital Healthcare System

My application focuses on improving healthcare appointment management aligned with SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being.

In the current healthcare environment, many patients still rely on manual appointment systems such as phone calls or physical registration at clinics. This creates several issues such as missed appointments, double booking, lack of reminders, and inefficient record management.

The MS Executive Health Care system is designed to solve these problems by providing a centralized mobile-based appointment management platform. Users can book appointments, view schedules, and manage their medical visits in a structured and organized system.

The system also improves communication between patients and healthcare providers, reduces administrative workload, and enhances overall healthcare service efficiency. By digitizing the process, it ensures better accessibility, accuracy, and convenience for both users and healthcare staff.
